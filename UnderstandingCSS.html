About the Minecraft Bee

Bees are flying neutral mobs that live in bee nests and beehives.
If provoked, bees attack in a swarm to "sting" the player and inflict poison.

Bee Trivia

  • Bees are the only arthropod mob that can exist in Peaceful difficulty and are not necessary to
    necessary to earn the Monster Hunter or Monster Hunted advancements.
  • Bees were made to be half a block in size because Mojang considered this size to be the cutest.
  • Bees pollinate the top half of sunflowers.
